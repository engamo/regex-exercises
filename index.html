Regex Exercises

Exercise 1

Regexp Golf

Regular expressions for each of the given patterns:

  1. 1. `car and cat`: Regular Expression: `ca[rt]`
  2. 2. `pop and prop`: Regular Expression: `p[op]`
  3. 3. `ferret, ferry, and ferrari`: Regular Expression: `ferr(et|y|ari)`
  4. 4. `Any word ending in ious`: Regular Expression: `\b\w*ious\b`
  5. 5. `A whitespace character followed by a period, comma, colon, or semicolon`: Regular Expression: `\s[.,:;]`
  6. 6. `A word longer than six letters`: Regular Expression: `\b\w{7,}\b`
    7. 7. `A word without the letter e (or E)`: Regular Expression: `\b[^eE]+\b`

Exercise 2

Quoting Style

let message = "Hi, 'What do you do for a living?,' hmmm, 'I am a developer!'";

message = message.replace(/'([^']+)'/g, '"$1"');

console.log(message);

output: "Hi, "What do you do for a living?," hmmm, "I am a developer""
